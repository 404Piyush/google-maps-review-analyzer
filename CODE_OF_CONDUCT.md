# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respectful disagreement
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing appreciation for contributions

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Public or private harassment of any community member
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

Violations should be reported to **piyushutkar123@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly.
